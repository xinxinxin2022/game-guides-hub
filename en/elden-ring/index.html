💡 Quick Tips for New Players

  • Explore before you fight. Many bosses become trivial if you level up and find better gear first.
  • Use Spirit Ashes. They're not "cheating" — they're a core mechanic designed to help you.
  • Upgrade your weapon before your stats. A +10 weapon does more damage than +10 Strength.
  • Read item descriptions. FromSoftware hides critical quest information in item tooltips.
