# Best Pals to Use Long-Term in Palworld

One of the most common questions from new Palworld players is: which Pals are actually worth investing in for the long haul? With over 300 Pals in the game, it is easy to waste resources on creatures that get outclassed quickly. This guide breaks down the best Pals for every role — riding, combat, work, and farming — based on community-tested strategies and endgame viability.

Whether you are in your first hour or pushing toward endgame bosses, these are the Pals that carry you from start to finish.

## Pals You Can Use Forever

Some Pals are so versatile and powerful that they remain relevant from the moment you catch them through to your final endgame lineup. Invest in these first.

### Penking (皮皮鸡) — The Lifetime Ranch Worker

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Role | Ranch work (Egg production) |
| Best passives | Craftsmanship, Workaholic |
| Longevity | From day 1 to endgame |

Penking is the single most important worker Pal in the game. Egg production scales with star level, and a 4-star Penking with good work passives will produce Eggs for your entire playthrough. Catch as many as possible early and breed for the best passives.

### Reindox (波霸牛) — Milk Production

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Role | Ranch work (Milk production) |
| Best passives | Craftsmanship, Workaholic |
| Longevity | Permanent |

Like Penking, Reindox is a ranch staple. Milk is essential for Cake production, and Reindox output scales with stars. Prioritize catching high-star Reindox in the wild.

### Beeguard (骑士蜂) — Honey Production

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Role | Ranch work (Honey production) |
| Best passives | Craftsmanship, Workaholic |
| Longevity | Permanent |

Honey is the bottleneck for many Cake recipes. Beeguard output is purely star-dependent, so focus on catching and upgrading high-star specimens.

### Cloud Deer / Verdash (云海鹿) — The Best Ground Mount

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Type | Neutral |
| Role | Ground mount, dungeon runner |
| Key traits | Small hitbox (fits through dungeon doors), double jump |
| Best passives | Swift, Infinite Stamina, Legend, Noble |

Verdash is the community's consensus best ground mount. Its small hitbox means it never gets stuck on dungeon doors, and its double-jump reaches places other mounts cannot. Ideal passives: 2× stamina + 2× speed (Infinite Stamina + Swift + Legend + Noble).

Also useful for crossing oceans into the endgame oil rig area.

### Celestia (天擒鸟) — Base Transportation God

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Type | Neutral |
| Role | Base mobility, combat dodging |
| Key traits | Fastest base runner, excellent at dodging boss skills |

Celestia is underrated but essential. Inside your base, it is the fastest Pal for moving between stations. In combat, its agility makes it the best mount for dodging boss attacks.

## Best Combat Pals

### Early Game (Levels 1-20)

- **Flame Breeze (烽歌龙)**: Good damage, easy to catch, useful for breeding later
- **Inverdale/Arsox**: Solid melee damage for their level

### Mid Game (Levels 20-40)

| Pal | Type | Why |
|-----|------|-----|
| **Anubis** | Neutral | Best manual worker + mining + carrying; also great combat with 3-Wind skill setup |
| **Night Hawk (夜幕魔蝠)** | Dark | Strong combat Pal available at Level 20+; one of the four Legendary passives must come from late-game beasts |

### Late Game / Endgame (Levels 40+)

| Pal | Type | Why |
|-----|------|-----|
| **Griffin (异构格里芬)** | Neutral | Double Disaster skill is arguably the strongest in the game; no Pal can tank it |
| **Typhon (泰锋)** | Dragon | One of the strongest Pals overall; Gap Tide + Thunderbolt + Dragon Slash skillset |
| **Moonfin (辉月依) / Frostallion (霜牙王)** | Ice/Dark | Top-tier combat Pals |

### Community Combat Tier List

1. **Griffin** — Double Disaster breaks the game
2. **Typhon** — Gap Tide + Thunderbolt + Dragon Breath
3. **Moonfin / Frostallion** — High base stats + strong skills
4. **Anubis** — Versatile (combat + work + mining)

**Key skill combo for endgame**: Gap Tide (间隙潮) + Thunderbolt (雷击) + either Rending Dragon Breath or Cutting Dragon Breath. Avoid pairing with Fire skills — damage drops significantly.

## The "Big 5" Passive Pals You Need Early

Experienced players recommend building five Pals with **Noble + Legendary** passives early in the game. These five become your permanent "power boost" Pals that you carry through the entire playthrough.

The exact Pals depend on your playstyle, but the principle is: catch Pals with Noble and Legendary passives as soon as possible and never replace them.

## Server Setup Tips for Pal Management

- **Catch a green-robed merchant Pal** at your base: one that sells Eggs/Milk, one that sells ammo, one black market merchant. This eliminates the need to visit villages.
- **Pal Terminal trick**: Place a second Pal Terminal near a boss arena or resource node. If you die, you respawn there instead of losing your spawn target. This is essentially a manual save point.
- **Disable death penalty** in single-player settings for free teleports via suicide (ESC → die → respawn at bed).

## FAQ

### Should I invest in shiny Pals or boss Pals?

Shiny and boss-template Pals have higher base stats, but the IV difference is small compared to what breeding can achieve. For combat, boss Pals with 50+ IV floor make excellent breeding stock. For work, focus on passives over shininess.

### What about the new Sakura Island Pals?

Sakura Island (added in the 1.0 update) introduces new Pals with unique skill trees. The Gap Tide + Thunderbolt combo from this area is currently the strongest damage combination in the game. Prioritize visiting once you have a flying mount.

### Are MODs worth installing for breeding?

Absolutely. The **IV Display MOD** and **Pal Terminal Expansion MOD** are essential for serious breeding. They let you see exact IVs and manage more Pals at your base.

## Conclusion

The best Pals in Palworld are the ones that stay relevant across multiple game phases. Penking, Reindox, and Beeguard are lifetime ranch workers. Verdash is the best ground mount you will ever need. Anubis is the Swiss Army knife of workers. And Griffin is the endgame combat king.

Focus your resources on these Pals first, then branch out to specialized breeds as your base infrastructure grows.
